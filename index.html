RULES

GOAL: Free your hand of all cards.

GAMEPLAY: Match the shape or color of the top card. Once you pick a shape or color, you must stick to it for that turn.

DRAWING: If you can't play, draw 3 cards and skip your turn. The direction reverses.

CARD TYPES

Regular Card: A Card with two characteristics, Shape and Color.

Single Card: A Card with only one characteristic, Shape or Color.

Elements Card: Wildcard. Can be played on anything. You choose the next color and shape.
